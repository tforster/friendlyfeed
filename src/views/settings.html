About

Version 0.1.0

Inspired by FriendFeed, the amazing social aggregator that Facebook bought and subsequently shutdown without incorporating any of its cool features into Facebook.

This version does nothing other than hide sponsored posts. Since Facebook is either incapable of serving interesting ads, or perhaps prefers the blatent carpet-bombing approach, this extension simply deletes them from the DOM
